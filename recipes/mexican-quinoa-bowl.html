Mexican Quinoa

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Onion, Diced
  • 1 Jalapeno, Seeded and Minced
  • 1 Cup Quinoa, Rinsed
  • 1 Cup Vegetable Broth
  • 1 (15 Ounce) Can Black Beans, Drained and Rinsed
  • 1 (14. Ounce) Can Fire Roasted Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 Cup Frozen Corn
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • 2 Teaspoons Chili Powder
  • ½ Teaspoon Cumin
  • ½ Teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste
  • Cubed Avocado and Parsley for serving

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Add onion, garlic, and jalapeno and cook until softened. About 2 minutes.
  2. Add black beans, tomatoes, corn, quinoa, vegetable broth, chili powder, cayenne, cumin, and lime juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper and stir.
  3. Bring to a simmer and reduce heat to medium low. Simmer for 20 minutes with the lid on stirring occasionally.
  4. Serve with diced avocado and fresh parsley.
  5. Enjoy!
